# 🎬 CineBook - Your Ultimate Movie Booking Companion

**Live Demo:** https://cinebook-omega.vercel.app

---

## 📖 About This Project

CineBook is a complete movie booking platform I built to bring the cinema experience to your fingertips. Whether you're a casual moviegoer or a hardcore cinephile, this platform makes booking tickets as enjoyable as watching the movies themselves.

I started this project with a simple idea - make movie booking feel like magic. Every feature, every animation, every interaction is designed to make you feel the excitement of going to the movies, right from your first click.

---

## 🎯 What You Can Do Here

### 🎟️ **Discover Movies**
Browse through a carefully curated collection of movies. Each movie card gives you a quick glance at what matters - rating, release year, duration, and genre. Hover over any movie to see more details or click to dive deeper.

### 📽️ **Watch Trailers**
Before you book, experience the movie. Every movie page has a trailer button that pulls the official trailer straight from YouTube. No leaving the site, no distractions - just pure cinema.

### 🎫 **Book Tickets**
Found something you like? Pick your date, choose your seats, and complete your booking. The entire process is smooth, fast, and designed to get you to the good part - the movie.

### ❤️ **Save Favorites**
See a movie you want to watch later? One click saves it to your favorites. Come back anytime, and your list is right there waiting.

### 🎙️ **Voice Assistant**
This one's special. Don't feel like typing? Just click the microphone and say what you want. "Take me home", "Show me movies", "Scroll down" - it listens and does exactly what you ask. It's like having a friend who understands cinema language.

### 👤 **Your Account**
Sign in with your Google account or email. Your profile, your favorites, your bookings - everything stays safe and syncs across devices.

### 📱 **Bookings & Tickets**
All your confirmed bookings live in one place. Click "View Ticket" to see your digital ticket - it's beautifully designed and ready to show at the cinema. Need a physical copy? Download or print it right from there.

### 📱 **Mobile Friendly**
Whether you're on your phone, tablet, or desktop, CineBook looks and works beautifully. The interface adapts to your screen, giving you the best experience no matter what device you're using.

## 🛠️ What Powers This

### **Frontend Stack**
- **React** - The core framework that makes everything feel smooth and responsive
- **Tailwind CSS** - For that clean, modern look without writing tons of custom CSS
- **Framer Motion** - Every animation you see - the glowing effects, smooth transitions, hover states - this library makes it all possible
- **Lucide React** - Beautiful icons that make navigation intuitive
- **React Router** - Seamless page transitions without reloading

### **Authentication & User Management**
- **Clerk** - Handles sign-ins, user profiles, and session management. It's secure, fast, and gives users a familiar login experience

### **Data & API Integration**
- **TMDB (The Movie Database)** - All movie data comes from here. Posters, backdrops, ratings, cast information - it's the heart of our movie catalog
- **Custom Backend API** - Handles bookings, favorites, and user data. All your information stays safe and organized

### **Features You'll Love**
- **Responsive Design** - Works on everything from iPhone to 4K monitors
- **Lazy Loading** - Images load as you scroll, keeping things fast
- **Smooth Scroll** - Clicking any link feels natural and fluid
- **Toast Notifications** - Friendly pop-ups that tell you what's happening
- **PDF Ticket Generation** - Download or print your tickets with one click
